About SkyGuard

Founded in Zomba by Fortune Kamphinda, uniting environmental action & tech expertise.

Fortune Kamphinda
Founder & CEO
Fortune Kamphinda is a University of Malawi Geography student, technology enthusiast and environmental innovator based in Lilongwe. With a strong interest in Desktop app development, software systems, and geospatial applications, he focuses on building practical digital solutions that address real world challenges. He is known for developing GeoWeatherMonitor(GWM) App which focuses on Weather worldwide, disaster preparation and now users can analyse terrain and know the flood prone areas using contours and 3D maps. Additionally he has also developed other systems like CGT System(Comprehensive GPS Tracking System) which helps in mapping as it collects cordinates remotely from smartphones to office computers automatically. His work spans environmental awareness initiatives and technical services, combining modern web technologies with system development to create impactful, community oriented projects.
